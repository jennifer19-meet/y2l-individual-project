ABOUT US



Pic n Choose was founded in 2019 as part of a project that has the potential to impact the surrounding communtiy. We wanted to create a website where anyone, professional or not, could upload and sell the photos they took. This is meant to help people who might need extra cash but it's also meant to provide aspiring and professional photographers with a platform that could help them expand their business. We also wanted to involve helping needy communities and good organizations which is why we require that every image sold has a percentage of its price donated to a charity of the buyer choice.
