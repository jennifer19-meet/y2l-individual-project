What Am I?

“Pic ‘n choose” is a very modern and user friendly platform aimed to help people find photographs at a reasonable price while also helping a charity of their choice. It also aims to help professional and amateur photographers sell their work while also doing good for the world. Users can simply upload the image they want to sell, set the price and pick the percentage that goes to charity. Then our image recognition feature will help produce tags to help other users find the image easily and quickly.
